Latvia

A small country by the Baltic Sea

Latvia is a country in the Baltic region of Northern Europe. It is known for its nature, culture, cities and traditions.

Capital city

Riga is the capital of Latvia.

Riga is also one of the best-known cultural and historical destinations in Latvia.

Language

The official language is Latvian.


"Sveiki!" = Hello.
"Paldies!" = Thank you!

Nature

Latvia is often introduced through its forests, rivers, lakes and coastline.

Over half of Latvia is covered with forests. There are 12000+ rivers, 3000 lakes!

Culture

There are recorded over 1.2 million Latvian folk songs (dainas).
The Latvian Song and Dance Festival is one of the world's largest amateur music and dance celebrations. It is recognized by UNESCO as an important part of humanity’s cultural heritage.

40,000+ singers & dancers!
Sources: Latvia Travel, Mission Latvia, Interesting facts about Latvia
Aizpute

Our town: Aizpute

Aizpute is one of the oldest towns in Western Latvia. It's a place where history meets silence, inspiring nature and active community life.

Historic identity

Aizpute has a long history, because the town is about 777 years old. It has ancient wooden architecture and narrow streets.

Landscape

The town is located by the Tebra River, which is an important part of the local landscape.

Strong Community

Aizpute glorify heritage through local events, festivals and cultural activities that connect generations and strengthen community bonds.

Sources: Aizpute.lv, Visit Aizpute
